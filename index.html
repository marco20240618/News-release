What’s in 1.0

  • 10 news sources: AP, Reuters, CNN, Guardian, Axios, USA Today, ABC, CBS, NBC, 联合早报
  • Source picker (top-right) and scrollable section tab bar under the title
  • Headline rows show publish time + 3-line article preview
  • Long-press a headline for in-line preview + Share / Copy / Open actions
  • Article page: publish time, selectable text, adjustable font / size / line spacing
  • Pull-to-refresh, on-disk + in-memory cache
  • JavaScript-rendered sources (Axios, USA Today) loaded via headless WKWebView
